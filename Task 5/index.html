ABOUT US



The working of Non-governmental organizations (NGOs) and Non-profit organization (NPO) is almost same. We are working towards building a better platform for Humankind. The aim of our NGO can be a charity, any open service (may be organized as a not-for-profit corporation), as a trust, a cooperative (supporting organization), or they exist informally to work for humanity.Humanitarian voices their concerns, so they can lead secure & dignified lives. It works throughout pune. NGO consist of 82 small childrens,60 senior citizens & 50 womens. The donations are used for the childrens education and it also help to overall NGO Some people volunteering the NGO help us lot to develop our NGO.Workshop for taloring heald in ngo helped us lot in developing the charity foundation.

Charity Jet is a secular, not-for-profit organization in India, registered under the Societies Registration Act of 1860. Set up in 2015, the organization works for ‘the betterment of future of childrens & elder persons’ Humanitarian envisions a society where childrens and elderly have the right to an active, healthy and dignified life. This NGO is working for education of children for thier bright future.
